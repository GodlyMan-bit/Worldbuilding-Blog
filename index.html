Welcome to Aurelia

A living journal of maps, lore, and stories. I add a little every week or two — email me your .md posts and I will drop them into _posts and update the site to include your submission.

About the Project

This project collects Aurelia's lore: history, places, songs, and short fiction. Posts are Markdown files with YAML frontmatter. The YAML frontmatter helps categorize all the posts. The reader displays Date, Author, and Tags at the top of each entry.

This blog is not describing the world; it is discovering it.
