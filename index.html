Welcome!

Hello I am Jude Krusemark. This is my personal blog where I post my personal essays, musings, and other such things. I am unapologetically Lutheran and have deep interest in hymnody and music, especially the likes of Bach and Buxtehude.

Soli Deo Gloria

About the Project

This project utilizes Jeckyll, an open-source stactic site generator (SSG) that transforms plain text files (like markdown) into complete ready to use webpages. All the posts go into a folder and then get displayed on the site. The reader displays Date, Author, and Tags at the top of each entry.
